Update 0.2.0 Notes - VTuber Rush!

2 weeks ago - Written by Gio Ciudadano

Game Updates

Update 0.2.0 is here! This update focuses on reinforcing the identities of each character, and the addition of new reactions when combining different characters. New enemies will now also spawn in later waves so players will have a better challenge while playing.

SYSTEMS
Debuff: Applied by certain characters.
Reaction: Applied when two or more debuffs hit the same enemy. Consumes the applying debuffs.

CHARACTERS
Finana
- Slow reduced from 60% for 2.5 seconds to 50% decaying over 2 seconds
- Applies the 'Wet' debuff for the slow duration. If the target is 'Chilled', 'Frozen' reaction occurs.

Petra
- Slow reduced from 25% while in-zone to 25% decaying over 2 seconds while in-zone
- Applies the 'Chilled' debuff while in-zone. If the target is 'Wet', 'Frozen' reaction occurs.
- No longer damages and stuns enemies for 0.75 seconds.

Selen
- Dragon Origin: Attacks now explode in the target area, dealing 25% of the damage to surrounding enemies.
- Applies the 'Cursed' debuff to all enemies hit, including secondary targets.
- 'Cursed' debuff changed from 5 + 1% per second for 4 seconds to 8 + 0.75% per second for 3 seconds.
- Range reduced, projectile speed increased

REACTIONS
Frozen
- Stand in place for 1.25 seconds and receive 20% more damage from all attacks. (8 second cooldown)
- At the end of the freeze time, receive additional damage.

MAP
- Decreased enemy weight on waves 1-5
- Increased enemy weight on waves 16-25
- Added new waves 26-30, no new enemies or enemy types

Thank you for your continued support!

Ciudad Games


Ciudad Games is a public platform for students and young kids to start learning fundamental skills while playing video games. These games are written in HTML5 and JavaScript, ensuring that users can play using only a browser without the need for downloads.

This web application is a conceptual framework created by Gio Carlo Ciudadano as a partial requirement for his course in CMSC 126. The images, videos, and logos used in this application are all licensed under CC-0 or Creative Commons, allowing the student to use such assets for educational purposes only.
