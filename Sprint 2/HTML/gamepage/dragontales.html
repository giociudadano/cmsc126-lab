Dragon Tales

Coming Soon

More Information

Ciudad Games is releasing its second browser-ready title, Dragon Tales, on June 6, 2022.

Dragon Tales is an action-adventure role-playing game where you take control as an adventurer ready to set forth to the world of dragons. Take control of one of four different classes, and battle waves of enemies, or train them to be your friend. Take control of one of up to a hundred different combinations of familiars, each with their own strengths and weaknesses. Use your familiars during battle to level them up or find even stronger familiars during your journey.

It's a fun game for young kids and adults alike!

Ciudad Games


Ciudad Games is a public platform for students and young kids to start learning fundamental skills while playing video games. These games are written in HTML5 and JavaScript, ensuring that users can play using only a browser without the need for downloads.

This web application is a conceptual framework created by Gio Carlo Ciudadano as a partial requirement for his course in CMSC 126. The images, videos, and logos used in this application are all licensed under CC-0 or Creative Commons, allowing the student to use such assets for educational purposes only.
